UC Davis students evaluate teaching assistants (TAs) on 5 qualities, each on a 5-point Likert scale. They can also optionally write an open-ended comment about their TAs (see below for some samples)

So far, I have taught 324 students across 6 terms, with 127 students opting to fill out evaluations (39.20%). My average rating across all students and questions is 4.55 out of 5 (between "very good" and "excellent").

PDF versions of the course evaluations are available upon request.
