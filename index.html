About Me:

I am a PhD student in Linguistics at the University of California, Davis, where I work in the Phonetics Lab with Dr. Georgia Zellou.

My academic interests include phonetics/phonology, human-computer interaction, pragmatics, and sociolinguistics.



I am currently a co-chair of the Cluster on Language Research at UC Davis, an interdisciplinary organization which holds biweekly talks on language research and an annual symposium in May. Click here to learn more about our events!
