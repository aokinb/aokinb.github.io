About Me:

I am a PhD student in Linguistics at the University of California, Davis, where I work in the Phonetics Lab with Dr. Georgia Zellou.

My academic interests include phonetics/phonology, speech perception, sociolinguistics, and human-computer interaction. Please click here to access my full CV.
